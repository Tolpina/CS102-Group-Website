My Start In Politics

I began my start in politics after the mayor of Bellingham submitted a leave of absence and then mysteriously disappeared... I was the Mayor of Bellingham for 4 years, and although it was a good foundation for the beginning of my political journey I wanted to impact the world on a grander scale. During my years of being mayor I had a very overall positive rating and successfully passed all of my legislation by the city counsel. Although I seemed to be successful in my political career as a mayor I left my position and was elected to the U.S senate on november 4th, 2014 and have been actively serving tell present. I also had a supportive backing in my decisions in the senate, as I am not totally loyal to either the republican and democratic parties and I vote on legislation according to my personal values and if I think it will benefit the majority. Taking into account what I have learned during my time as mayor and a senator and all that I have learned as I've grown, I'm ready to face new horizons and fight bigger battles to provide Americans a truly just and coherent society. My presidency will break the barriers that have been restricting America from its true potential.
