WHAT IS ANARCHY?

+ + +

Although most people have heard the term "anarchy," many don't understand what it actually means — especially in the context of today's political system. Gabe describes his version of anarchy in modern terms:

"Think of Anarchy as the next step past Socialism, except without any of the problems. Under an Anarchist reign, everyone is equal; I plan to strip away all corruption and unfairness through the abolishment of Democracy altogether. It's not Democracy, not Socialism — it's a complete lack of any sort of governmental system," he explains. "The only one in a position of power will be me, but since no government really exists, I will hold no authority over the common people. We basically all just do whatever we want, and everyone is on equal ground."

When elected, Gage plans to implement a number of Anarchist policies during his four-year term. If all goes well, America will achieve an Anarchist Utopia by 2024!
